About Us

Welcome to our website! Our project involved a blend of various technologies and skills, including HTML, web scraping, Python, and website creation.

Our Process

Throughout the development of this project, we encountered numerous challenges and opportunities for growth. One of the most challenging aspects was learning HTML from scratch to design and structure this website. HTML served as the backbone of our project, allowing us to create a visually appealing and user-friendly interface.

In addition to HTML, we delved into the realm of web scraping using Python. This involved extracting player data from various online sources and integrating it into our project. The process of web scraping opened up new possibilities and deepened our understanding of data manipulation and automation.

Furthermore, we honed our skills in website creation, experimenting with different ideas and tools to bring our vision to life.
